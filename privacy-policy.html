Privacy Policy for Beck Auto-Post Chrome Extension

Last Updated: December 30, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the Beck Auto-Post Chrome extension ("Extension", "we", "our") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

What We Collect:

  • Vehicle Inventory Data: The Extension processes vehicle inventory data from CSV files that you provide or sync from your server. This data includes: VIN numbers, vehicle make/model/year, pricing, and other inventory information. This data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
  • Extension Settings: Your extension configuration (CSV URL, API keys, preferences) is stored locally in your browser.
  • Usage Data: The Extension does not collect or transmit usage analytics or telemetry data.

How We Use Data

Local Processing:

  • All vehicle data and settings are stored and processed locally in your browser.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers.
  • Data remains on your device or your designated server.

AI Service Integration:

  • When generating descriptions, the Extension sends vehicle information to AI services (OpenAI or Anthropic) using YOUR API key.
  • This data transmission is controlled by you through your API key.
  • We do not have access to your API keys or the data sent to AI services.

CSV Synchronization:

  • The Extension fetches CSV data from your designated server (configured by you).
  • This connection is between your browser and your server.
  • We do not have access to this data or connection.

Data Storage

Local Storage:

  • Vehicle inventory data is stored locally in Chrome's storage.
  • Settings and preferences are stored locally.
  • Data persists on your device until you clear browser data or uninstall the extension.

No Cloud Storage:

  • We do not store your data on our servers.
  • We do not have access to your data.
  • All data remains under your control.

Third-Party Services

AI Services (OpenAI/Anthropic):

  • When you use AI description generation, vehicle data is sent to these services using YOUR API key.
  • These services have their own privacy policies.
  • You control this interaction through your API key configuration.
  • We recommend reviewing their privacy policies:
    • OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
    • Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

Facebook Marketplace:

  • The Extension interacts with Facebook Marketplace to create listings.
  • This interaction is subject to Facebook's privacy policy.
  • We do not collect or store Facebook data.

Data Security

  • All data processing happens locally in your browser.
  • API keys are stored locally and encrypted by Chrome.
  • We do not transmit your data to our servers.
  • You are responsible for securing your API keys and server access.

Your Rights

Access:

  • You can view all stored data through Chrome's extension storage.
  • All data is accessible to you locally.

Deletion:

  • Uninstall the extension to remove all local data.
  • Clear Chrome storage to remove extension data.
  • You control your CSV data on your server.

Control:

  • You control what data is processed (through CSV file selection).
  • You control API key usage.
  • You control server connections.

Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be reflected in the extension updates and this document. The "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
